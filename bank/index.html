Vandals Bank


Vandals Bank is a token distribution application


Stake ZOMBIE and MAZE in Vandals Bank*

You can stake any amount of ZOMBIE and max 100k MAZE tokens at any time in the Vandals Bank.

Staking rewards distribution:
- 14% of the ZOMBIE amount staked after 7 days - reward is VANDALS,
- 7% of MAZE amount staked after 7 days - reward is UNDERGROUND (URGD).

For the first time you can claim reward immediately after placing your stake (no need to wait 7 days).
You have to wait 7 days to claim reward again.
You can claim staking reward later (e.g. in a month), but you will receive 14% (when staking ZOMBIE) or 7% (when staking MAZE) of your balance staked, but
if you claim every week you can get up to 730% or 365% per year.

You can unstake at any time.

You need Bitcoin Cash for blockchain transaction fee.
A small amount of BCH (0.00002) is included in the transaction fee at the time the URGD claim is executed. It is used to repurchase and increase liquidity.

You must have MetaMask wallet (or other wallet) configured for smartBCH (Bitcoin Cash sidechain).
Reload the page if Vandals Bank is not connected to MetaMask or "undefined".

See Faucet - claim ZOMBIE and URGD.
