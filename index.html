Token mining requires competition and resources in exchange for reward

Maze utilizes modified open-source Mist miner, operates without a central issuer
and offers fast peer-to-peer transactions in Bitcoin Cash blockchain

Maze Simple Tokenomics:

No authority, person or company control Maze supply. Mint baton is stored in p2sh vault address. The smart contract will not allow total supply to be higher than 21 million
Risk: supply cannot be easily manipulated, but there may be a disparity in the amount of tokens mined

In general Maze does not offer an „investing plan” or „dividends”. The price of the token is a free market price
Risk: temporary low liquidity, price dumping by active miners

The main target markets of Maze are digital arts and collectibles
Risk: Maze may be less popular than heavily advertised and promoted tokens
