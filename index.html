MAZE

MAZE is a mineable cryptocurrency token based on Mist proof-of-work covenant contract and Bitcoin script
Maze operates in Bitcoin Cash blockchain and is powered by Simple Ledger Protocol

Mineable-Autonomous-Decentralized (M-A-D)

Maze operates without a central issuer and the token supply can not be easily manipulated
Maze is for creative people and can be used in various projects
We assume that MAZE is the second mineable SLP Token Type 1 ever created and the first mineable SLP NFT1-Group Token
