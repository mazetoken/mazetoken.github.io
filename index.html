MAZE SLP Token

MAZE is a mineable (proof-of-work) Simple Ledger Protocol token. It is based on Mistcoin spedn smart contract and Bitcoin script.

Mineable-Autonomous-Decentralized (M-A-D)

MAZE operates without a central issuer and offers fast peer-to-peer transactions in Bitcoin Cash blockchain.

The token development is privately funded and its creator does not offer „investing plans”, „dividends” or "staking rewards".
That is not what MAZE is supposed to be.

However MAZE is for creative people and can be used in various projects in a permissionless way.
The main target markets of MAZE are digital arts, collectibles and tokens of appreciation.
Risk: less popular than some other heavily advertised and promoted tokens.

No authority, person or company control MAZE supply. The smart contract will not allow total supply to be higher than 21 million.
Risk: the token supply can not be easily manipulated, but there may be a disparity in the amount of tokens mined.

The price of the token is a free market price.
Risk: limited exchange availability, temporary low liquidity, price dumping by active miners.

It is assumed that MAZE is the second mineable SLP Token Type 1 and the first mineable SLP NFT1-Group Token

    • MAZE SLP Token mining

    MAZE mining requires competition and resources in exchange for reward. Mining is also an innovative way to distribute SLP tokens.
    The token mining utilizes modified open-source Mistcoin miner, Electron Cash SLP wallet and BCHD based servers for mining.
    The token minting baton is stored in a p2sh vault address.

    Download Mminer from Github. Mining tutorial for Windows 10, Kali Linux or Ubuntu is included in the repository. You can also use the original Mistcoin miner or other miners available.

    Risk: the miner is not "out of the box" application, other software and a command line must be used to setup and use the miner, "faster" miners (with better hardware or BCHD full node) may dominate mining.
